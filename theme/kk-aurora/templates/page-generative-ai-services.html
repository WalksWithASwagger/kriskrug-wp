Engagement models

Pricing is scoped after a short discovery call.

A keynote, a board briefing, a community buildout, and a training cohort are different animals. The useful question isn't "what package can I sell you?" — it's "what change are we trying to make, and what shape gets us there?" Most engagements begin with one of these and then tighten around audience, urgency, budget, and implementation.

Advisory Sprint

Two to four weeks of discovery, interviews, strategy, and a usable roadmap.

Team Training / Cohort

Half-day, full-day, or multi-week training with demos, exercises, and follow-up resources.

Ecosystem Buildout

Six to twelve weeks of program design, partner mapping, event architecture, and operating rhythms.

Keynote + Workshop

A public talk paired with a practical lab so the energy turns into action.
