## Draft Angle

This should be the practical/transformation proof point. The core story is not "AI is coming." It is "KK can walk into an event, synthesize the room in real time, and turn a conference into a knowledge base while teaching people how the work actually changes."

## Suggested Structure

1. Open with the live-demo premise: synthesis happened during the event, not afterward.
2. Explain the practical stack: transcription, structured knowledge, personal assistants, open-source workflows.
3. Translate the business relevance: teams need learning systems, not one-off prompt tips.
4. Tie to workshops and custom training.
5. Embed the video.
6. Close with a CTA for event organizers, associations, and teams.
