## Do Not Publish Without Verification

Patterns excluded from live payloads:

- Generic praise with no named source.
- Testimonials that read like synthetic sales copy.
- Private client comments with no public permission.
- Long transcript fragments from workshops.
- Any testimonial copied from a private planning page without a screenshot, name, or public source.
