Engagement Models

Pricing is scoped after a short discovery call because a keynote, a board briefing, a community buildout, and a training cohort are different animals. The useful question is not "what package can I sell you?" It is "what change do we need the room, team, or ecosystem to make?"

Most engagements begin with one of these shapes and then get tightened around audience, urgency, budget, and implementation needs.

Good fit when you need

  • Executive clarity on AI adoption
  • A team training that produces actual workflows
  • A public room that leaves smarter and braver
  • A community program that can keep growing
  • Protocol-aware AI and data strategy

Advisory Sprint

Two to four weeks of discovery, interviews, strategy, and a usable roadmap.

Team Training / Cohort

Half-day, full-day, or multi-week training with demos, exercises, and follow-up resources.

Ecosystem Buildout

Six to twelve weeks of program design, partner mapping, event architecture, and operating rhythms.

Keynote + Workshop

A public talk paired with a practical lab so the energy turns into action.
