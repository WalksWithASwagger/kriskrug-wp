Ecosystem

BC + AI Ecosystem

As Executive Director, I help convene the public community, industry association, partners, and policy-facing rooms for responsible, inclusive, place-rooted AI in British Columbia.

  • Impact: 250+ members, 3,000+ event attendees, 94+ events, and 4 BC regions.
  • Outcome: a community-first AI association that gives artists, founders, researchers, policy people, and Indigenous leaders a shared table.

Indigenous tech

Indigenomics AI

CTO work at the intersection of Indigenous economic sovereignty, sovereign data, platform strategy, and Carol Anne Hilton's Indigenomics framework.

  • Impact: supports the $100B Indigenous economy vision with technology that serves evidence, governance, and self-determination.

Training

The Upgrade AI

Co-founded with Peter Bittner, The Upgrade AI turns AI literacy into hands-on courses, coaching, team trainings, and applied sessions for working professionals.

  • Impact: live and on-demand courses, certification cohorts, 1:1 and group coaching, and team trainings.
  • Outcome: helps organizations and creative professionals build useful AI fluency without losing judgment, taste, or responsibility.
