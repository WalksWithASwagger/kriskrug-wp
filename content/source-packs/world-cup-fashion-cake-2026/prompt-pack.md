# Prompt Pack

These are the first 20 text-to-image prompts used to calibrate the Becker x KK Vancouver World Cup 2026 fashion direction. They intentionally avoid official logos and use World Cup 2026 as cultural context, not trademarked art direction.

## Calibration Instructions

Use these prompts with a Becker mood board and a curated reference set. Start with the first five to ten prompts as a style calibration batch. Review outputs quickly, then adjust the mood board or prompt language before spending more generation budget.

Selection criteria:

- Hair has to be strong enough to justify Becker's name.
- Vancouver and LA should both be legible without becoming postcard cliches.
- Soccer references should feel couture/editorial, not fan merch.
- Prefer images with useful photographic structure: strong face, clean pose, interesting negative space, usable crop.
- Reject anything with official logos, broken anatomy, unreadable garments, fake text, or generic AI gloss.
