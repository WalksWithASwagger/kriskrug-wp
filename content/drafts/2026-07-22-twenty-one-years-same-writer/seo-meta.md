## Search intent

The article answers a practical question: can style checkers or AI detectors prove who wrote a piece of text? The answer is no. It shows what a voice lint tool can still do well and why provenance must be captured separately.

## Excerpt

I scored 1,274 recovered blog posts against the words I type today. The surprising result was not that AI sounds different. It was that a voice checker can catch an unedited machine, but cannot prove a human was ever there.
