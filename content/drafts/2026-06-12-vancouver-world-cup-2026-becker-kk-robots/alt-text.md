## Requirements Before Publish

- Alt text must describe the actual selected image, not the prompt that produced it.
- Do not mention "AI-generated" in every alt text unless it is contextually useful.
- Captions can carry process/provenance; alt text should serve readers first.
- Reference and process screenshots need clear labels if used publicly.
