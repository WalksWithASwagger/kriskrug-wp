I didn't set out to build a scene. I set out to find my people — the artists, hackers, researchers, founders, policy folks, and beautifully unreasonable builders who were already doing the work, long before the lanyards and the global conferences showed up. What grew out of that is the Vancouver AI community and the broader BC + AI ecosystem: a grassroots, people-first answer to the question of who actually gets to shape AI here.

This is the home base for that story — the monthly meetups, the recaps, the essays, and the receipts. If you're new, start at the top. If you've been here a while, you already know: you are the BC + AI ecosystem.
