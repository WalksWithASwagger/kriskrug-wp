# Accessibility Statement

Kris Krug (kriskrug.co) wants this site to be usable by as many people as possible, including people who use assistive technologies.

## Our commitment

We aim for the site to meet [WCAG 2.2 Level AA](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/quickref/) where practical. We test key public pages with automated checks and fix barriers when we find them. Accessibility work continues as the site evolves.

## What we have done

- Keyboard-accessible primary navigation and main call-to-action controls on current Aurora templates
- Skip-to-content links on public pages
- Ongoing contrast and focus work on high-traffic surfaces
- Prefer clear link text and plain language in new content

## Known limitations

- Some older posts and media embeds may still have incomplete captions, transcripts, or alt text
- Complex layouts and third-party embeds (newsletter, video, maps) can be harder for assistive tech
- PDF or external assets linked from posts may not meet the same standard as HTML pages
- A full site-wide WCAG audit is not claimed; work is iterative
