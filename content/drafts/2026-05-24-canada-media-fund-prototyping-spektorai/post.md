**Project Title**: Spektor.AI: AI-Powered Mental Health Platform

### **1. Project Overview**

**Project Summary**: Spektor.AI is an innovative AI-powered mental health platform designed to provide personalized, accessible, and engaging mental health support. The platform features AI-driven journaling, multifaceted expert analysis, personalized growth plans, continuous engagement, and therapist integration. It aims to bridge the gap between digital mental health tools and traditional therapy, offering a unique and engaging user experience that promotes long-term mental well-being.

**Key Features**:

AI-Driven Journaling ChatbotMultifaceted Expert AnalysisPersonalized Growth PlansContinuous Engagement and Follow-upsTherapist IntegrationGamified User Experience

### **2. Project Status and Objectives**

**Current Status**:

An initial version of Spektor.AI has been developed and tested over the past three months with positive feedback from psychology professionals.The platform has a working prototype that includes core features like AI-driven journaling and personalized recommendations.

**Objectives for Prototyping Funding**:

Refine and expand the existing prototype to enhance user experience and engagement.Test and validate new features, such as advanced gamification and therapist integration tools.Address user feedback to improve AI algorithms and ensure compliance with mental health standards.Develop an enhanced UX/UI design to make the platform more intuitive and engaging.

### **3. Market Potential and Innovation**

**Market Opportunity**:

The global digital mental health market is projected to reach $88.9 billion by 2027. Spektor.AI addresses a critical need for accessible mental health support and bridges the gap between AI-driven tools and traditional therapy.

**Innovation**:

Spektor.AI&#x27;s unique combination of AI-driven journaling, personalized insights, and therapist integration sets it apart from competitors. The platform’s gamified approach makes mental health management engaging and approachable, which is rare in the current market.

### **5. Detailed Project Plan**

**Goals**:

Develop a fully functional prototype that incorporates new features.Validate technical feasibility and user engagement through iterative testing.Establish a clear pathway from prototype to full-scale product launch.

**Timeline**:

**Months 1-2**: Refine core features based on initial feedback and prepare new UX/UI elements.**Months 3-4**: Conduct beta testing with targeted user groups, including psychology professionals and potential end-users.**Months 5-6**: Finalize the prototype, addressing feedback, and optimizing for user engagement.

**Budget**:

Breakdown of costs, including software development, testing, UX/UI design, and marketing for the prototype phase.

### **6. Evaluation and Outcomes**

**Success Metrics**:

User engagement rates (time spent on platform, frequency of use).Positive feedback from professionals on AI insights and usability.Achieving a functional prototype that can be presented to investors and healthcare stakeholders.

**Expected Outcomes**:

A refined prototype ready for market launch phases.Clear evidence of market fit and user need, supported by robust testing data.A platform poised to revolutionize the mental health support landscape.

### **7. Financial Projections and Funding Needs**

**Funding Request**: $2.5 million to complete the prototyping phase, including:

Finalizing development of core features.Launching marketing initiatives for initial user acquisition.Scaling operations based on prototype feedback and engagement metrics.
